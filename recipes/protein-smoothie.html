Protein Smoothie

Description

A low calorie, high protein smoothie that tastes delicious and can be used to supplement protein intake and speed up muscle recovery, or just if you're in the mood for a tasty snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 scoops vanilla protein powder
  • 1 tbsp chopped walnuts
  • 1 cup of ice
  • 1 whole banana
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2cup water

Steps

  1. Pour the 1/2 cup of water into a blender jar.
  2. Cut the banna into small pieces and add them to the jar.
  3. Pour the 1/2 cup of milk into the jar.
  4. Add the ice to the jar.
  5. Then add in the tablespoon of peanut butter.
  6. Lastly add the chopped up walnuts.
  7. Blend for a total of 2 minutes on a setting of at least eight.
