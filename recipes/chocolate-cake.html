Chocolate Cake

Description

Sweet, moist, chocolate, cake. Perfect for any special occasion be it a special occasion like a birthday, wedding, or party.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 3 cups flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 cups water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees C. Grease and flour two nine inch round pans.
  2. Stir the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt all together in a large bowl. Then add the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla, mix for 2 minutes on medium speed of mixer.
  3. Stir in the boiling water last. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans.
  4. Bake 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
